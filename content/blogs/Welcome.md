## Hello World!

It's time. Time to start writing (in public) about my thoughts and experiences. With luck this will one day provide some perspective or knowledge to someone else in the field, but at the very least it can supplement my own memory.

Cyber security is a complex and wide ranging field. It encompasses a myriad of disciplines and can often be conflated with other similar, but fundamentally different fields and skillsets. Lessons can come from direct technical experience but they can (and should) also come from other more established fields. 

Skilled practitioners fuse their own experience with the experience of others to produce solutions needed where and when they are need. Hopefully, this will serve as a repository of experience to help you develop your own solutions.
